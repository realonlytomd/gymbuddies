How It Works

  • CREATE ACCOUNT
    Click on create account on the menu. Complete the fields to create an account.
    LOG IN
    Click on login on the menu bar and enter your username and password.
    LOG YOUR WORKOUT
    After you have logged in, you will be placed on the log page. Enter your planned workout by choosing a body part and exercise. Then enter your optional weight, sets, and reps.
    CHECK YOUR SUMMARY
    After planning your workout, click on the enter workout button and a summary of your workout will be shown.
